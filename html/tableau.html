Top Boating Sports

  1. Wake Surfing
    • A rider is pulled up onto the surfboard by the boat force. They then continue to trail the boat while surfing its wake and sometimes surfing without the rope!
  2. Water Skiing
    • The rider is pulled behind the boat while skimming the surface of the water. This sport can be done with a single or pair of ski(s).
  3. Wake Boarding
    • The rider's feet are strapped into a board that is able to skim the water when being pulled behind a boat. The rider is able to use the boat's wake to perform various tricks.
